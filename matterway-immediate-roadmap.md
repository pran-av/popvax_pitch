Strategic Alignment (Week 1)
Action: Conduct a "listening tour" with Cognizant BPO leads and Matterway’s founders.

The "Why": Ensure your definition of "impact" matches their expectations (e.g., is the priority reducing labor costs or improving customer NPS?).

Artifact: A "State of the Union" memo summarizing the gap between current capability and vision.

2. Metric & Driver Identification (Week 2)
Action: Map the "North Star" (e.g., Average Handling Time - AHT) to its technical drivers.

Example: * Metric: AHT for Insurance Claims.

Drivers: Search time for customer IDs, "swivel-chair" data entry between SAP and legacy web apps, and manual verification of PDF attachments.

3a. (Weeks 3-5)
High-Volume Cohort Identification 
Action: Since you lack deep data, use proxy data (e.g., seat count per client or ticket volume logs) to find the "Heavy Hitters."

Target: Focus on the 20% of processes that account for 80% of agent time, like KYC Sanctions Screening or Purchase Order administration.

3b. Pattern Recognition & Behavioral Insights
Action: Formulate "If-Then" hypotheses based on observed behaviors.

Examples of Insights:

The "Copy-Paste" Loop: "I observed that agents copy the same 'Customer ID' six times across four tabs. Insight: A universal 'Context Clip' could save 40 seconds per case."

The "SOP Hunt": "Agents keep a physical notebook of codes because the digital SOP is too slow to load. Insight: Embedding the SOP directly into the Assistant UI eliminates cognitive load."

The "Validation Wait": "Agents wait for a legacy system to load just to check if a checkbox is marked. Insight: Pre-fetching that data via an AI agent removes idle time."

6. Delivery & Measurement (Weeks 6-12)
Action: Ship a "No-Code" or "Low-Code" intervention (e.g., a simple UI overlay or automated trigger).

Measure: Compare the "Before" (recorded during shadowing) vs. the "After" for that specific cohort.

Deliverable: A "Quick-Win Report" showing a 10-15% improvement, providing the leverage needed for the long-term roadmap.
