# Strategy

The fronts to chronic diseases - 
1. Scope of treatments are wide and chances or survival increase if we detect early. So the most impactful front becomes - Early Detection and Prevention 
2. The second main front is to deal with the already existing invasion. And with that gain knowledge about the enemy. So primary goal of this front is to cure existing ailments but we have a secondary goal of developing technique and identify the best counter measures. - Cure and Technique
